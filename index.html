About Me

MENG-JU HSIEH | 謝孟儒

Hi, I am a master's researcher at ITBE@TUM. Focused on using IT & software development skills (C#, DBMS, BIM API & ML) to advance the digitalization of AEC industry. Leveraging engineering data, IOT & digital twin models to simulate, optimize and inform decision-making. My work aims to enhance the efficiency and quality of construction life-cycle management. I believe that technology is the key to offer resilient and sustainable solutions for today's challenges.
